Theme: Prosper.Sapience:

“Prosperity is a mindset. It is our expectation and quest for it.”

The evolution of the hominid species to modern-day sapiens took over two million years. Being the most evolved and intelligent species on planet Earth ,are humans worthy of the title the wise ones? Does the turbulence and unrest that we showcase show otherwise? Wisdom is the understanding of the self, being in peace with it and smothering the turbulent undercurrent that we all possess deep within ourselves. We are in search of stories and experiences that can enrich every listener out there and help them shape their perspective and incorporate ideas of progress and prosperity in their lives.

TED is a nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks (18 minutes or less). TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics - from science to business to global issues - and is covered in most of the languages in the world. TED stands for one mission and that is: Spread ideas.

TED is a global community, welcoming those people from every discipline and culture that passionately believe in the power of ideas to change attitudes, lives and, ultimately, the world. TED aims to build a clearinghouse of free knowledge from the world's most inspired thinkers and through that, bring forth a community of curious souls to engage with ideas and each other, both online and at TED and TEDx events around the world, all year long.

At its premier edition in 2018, with the theme of "Alchemy", TEDxTKMCE brought 8 illustrious speakers from across the world and from different stratas of society. Aiming to bust myths and unravel lustrous possibilities. . Following in its footsteps and unfazed by the greatest challenge our generation has faced till date, This year, We bring TEDxTKMCE Virtual. TEDx is a haven wherein thoughts are sown, ideas sprout and innovation thrives. Our mission is to use this virtuous dais to reach inward and exonerate the power within and truly bring out the best in our audience and make us a beacon of resilience in these difficult times.
